What the agent discovered— corners
01 — Represent
The track as data
The circuit is sampled into nodes; each carries a position, a normal, and the legal track width. The line is a lateral offset at every node.
02 — Evaluate
A physics lap clock
For any candidate line: curvature → max corner speed (grip), then forward/backward passes for braking & power. Sum the segment times = lap time.
03 — Search
Local-search optimizer
Each lap, the agent molds a smooth local bump in the line, re-clocks it, and keeps the change only if it's faster. Exploration cools over time.
04 — Explain
Co-pilot output
From the converged line it extracts apex side, braking points and minimum speeds — the pace notes a driver actually hears.
